At-large Candidates

Seventeen candidates are running to fill four at-large seats in the Council. Two incumbents, Councilor Michael Flaherty and Julia Mejia, are running for re-election while Michelle Wu and Annissa Essaibi George is running for mayor, leaving two slots up for grabs.
